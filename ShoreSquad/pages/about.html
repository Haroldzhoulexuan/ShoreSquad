About ShoreSquad

Rallying young people to protect our beaches, one cleanup at a time

Our Mission

ShoreSquad is more than just an app ‚Äì it's a movement. We believe that young people have the power to create lasting change for our planet's beaches and oceans. Our mission is to make beach cleanups accessible, engaging, and impactful by connecting communities through technology.

Every piece of trash removed from our shores is a step toward healthier marine ecosystems, cleaner beaches, and a more sustainable future for generations to come.
